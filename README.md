## üìã Executive Summary

QuickCook is a revolutionary mobile application that solves the age-old problem of "What should I cook with what I have?" Using advanced AI technology, QuickCook generates personalized recipes based on available ingredients, dietary preferences, and cooking skill level. The app combines the power of OpenAI's GPT models with comprehensive nutrition data and smart meal planning to create a complete cooking companion.

### üéØ Mission Statement
To democratize home cooking by making it accessible, enjoyable, and waste-free through intelligent recipe generation and comprehensive meal planning.

### üí° Vision
